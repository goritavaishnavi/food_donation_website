About FoodShare

Connecting hearts and plates to eliminate hunger, one meal at a time.

Our Mission

FoodShare is a revolutionary platform designed to bridge the gap between food surplus and food insecurity. We connect donors (restaurants, hotels, individuals) with NGOs and volunteers to ensure that surplus food reaches those who need it most, instead of going to waste.

What We Do

Our platform provides a seamless experience for all stakeholders in the food donation ecosystem. Donors can easily post available food, NGOs can request donations, and volunteers help transport food from donors to recipients. We handle the logistics, notifications, and coordination to make food donation as simple as possible.

Our Values

Compassion

Every action we take is driven by empathy and the desire to help those in need.

Trust

We build transparency through ratings, feedback, and verified user profiles.

Sustainability

By reducing food waste, we contribute to a more sustainable future for everyone.

Join Our Mission

Whether you're a restaurant with surplus food, an NGO working to feed the hungry, or a volunteer willing to help, there's a place for you in the FoodShare family. Together, we can make a difference in our communities.
